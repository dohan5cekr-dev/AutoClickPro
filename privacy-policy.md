# Privacy Policy for AutoClickPro

**Effective Date: May 24, 2024**

Welcome to **AutoClickPro**. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

## 1. Data Collection
Our application does not collect personally identifiable information (such as name, phone number, or email) on our servers. The data processed includes:
- **Click Coordinates:** Stored locally on your device to perform automated click functions.
- **Time Data (NTP):** We connect to public NTP servers to synchronize accurate atomic time.

## 2. Sensitive Permissions
To function correctly, the app requires the following permissions:
- **Root Access (ACCESS_SUPERUSER):** Used to perform simulated click actions and synchronize the system clock with high precision. We do not use this permission to access your personal files or sensitive data.
- **Display Over Other Apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW):** Required to display the floating control button.
- **Internet Access:** Used to fetch world time (NTP) and display advertisements.
- **Location Access (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION):** May be used by advertising partners to show relevant content.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. Google may collect anonymous data (such as Advertising IDs) to personalize ads. You can view their policy here: [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 4. Security
We implement security measures to protect information stored locally within the app. However, as this is a root-required application, users are responsible for the overall security of their rooted device.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be updated on this page.
